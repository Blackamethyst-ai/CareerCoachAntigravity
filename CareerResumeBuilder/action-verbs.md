## Usage Guidelines

### Verb Selection

Match verb category to role type:
- **Leadership roles:** Strategic verbs (Spearheaded, Drove, Championed)
- **Technical roles:** Execution verbs (Engineered, Automated, Deployed)
- **Sales roles:** Revenue verbs (Generated, Closed, Captured)
- **Operations roles:** Efficiency verbs (Streamlined, Optimized, Reduced)

### Avoid

- **Weak verbs:** Was responsible for, Helped, Assisted, Participated in
- **Passive voice:** Was managed by, Were implemented
- **Generic:** Worked on, Handled, Dealt with

### Vary Your Verbs

Don't repeat the same verb in consecutive bullets. Rotate through relevant categories to demonstrate range.
