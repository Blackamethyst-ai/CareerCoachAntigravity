## Red Flag Checklist

Before submission, verify:

- [ ] No unexplained gaps
- [ ] No typos or errors
- [ ] All links work
- [ ] Contact info complete
- [ ] Consistent formatting
- [ ] Professional email
- [ ] Every claim is defensible
- [ ] No desperation language
- [ ] Customized for this role
- [ ] Under 2 pages (unless justified)
